Meetings

PCASC meets @ 2249 E. Burnside on the 3rd Wednesday of every month, 7-9 pm. All are welcome, whether you’re returning to PCASC after a hiatus or are hoping to learn what we’re all about. Please contact us to add agenda items or to schedule to speak at an upcoming meeting: coreteam@pcasc.net or call and leave a message: 503-236-7916

Internships

Email coreteam@pcasc.net with all inquiries and to apply for an internship.
All internships require the following:

*4-6 hours per week
*3--6 month commitment
*Collaboration with PCASC staff
*Passion for social justice
*Excellent communication skills


All internships are unpaid, but request for academic credit will be considered.
