WHAT WE DO = WHY WE DO IT


1.


    PCASC raises resources to send to social movements in the Americas.


    equals


We have access to resources in part because of the exploitation of Latin American peoples and environment; we should use our access to provide resources whenever we can.



2.


    PCASC builds organizers and movement leaders through a decentralized, member-run organization, with support from skilled peers.


    equals


We believe that the movement must be built from the ground up and that there is strength in collective power. We believe that anti-oppression is critical to systemic change. We believe in developing and strengthening the political analysis and organizing skills of all PCASC members.



3.


    PCASC offers analysis and solutions that target the root causes of problems and demands systemic change while building power from the bottom towards a more just world.

    equals



We believe capitalism and oppression are the root causes of injustice.



    4.


    PCASC directly connects community members, students, and workers with their counterparts in the Americas.

    equals


    We believe that relationships and common struggles are critical foundations of solidarity.
